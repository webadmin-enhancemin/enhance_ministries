Mission Trip Partners

Free Coaching

When a pastor registers their church for a Student Mission Trip or Win Your Jerusalem experience, we come alongside them in a meaningful way.

Participating pastors receive free, once-a-month coaching sessions designed to encourage, equip, and support them as they lead their ministry and their mission team. It's our way of investing not only in students but in the leaders who shepherd them.
