The Crisis Facing Pastors

Pastors are the backbone of thriving churches, yet many face burnout, isolation, and overwhelming challenges alone.

22% of pastors receive regular support from peers or mentors
65% of pastors experience loneliness or isolation
19% of pastors say they have "true friends"

Sources: [1] Barna Group · [2] NCF/Barna

How Enhance Ministries Helps Ministry Leaders and Churches

Enhance Ministries exists to change these statistics. We come alongside pastors with personalized coaching, practical consulting, and genuine care so they can lead healthy, fruitful ministries for the long haul.

Coaching

One-on-one coaching and cohort experiences that provide encouragement, accountability, and practical tools for personal and ministry growth.

Church Consulting

Strategic guidance for church boards and ministry staff navigating organizational challenges. We help leadership teams develop healthy systems, clarify vision, and strengthen church governance.

Care Events

Intentional gatherings designed to refresh and restore pastors and their families. Through retreats, connection events, and pastoral care moments, we create space for rest and renewal.

Mission Trips

Engaging students, families, and churches in hands-on Kingdom work. From student missions to family experiences, we connect you with ministry partners in the Twin Cities for life-changing service opportunities.

Speaking & Training

Equipping conferences, retreats, and workshops led by experienced ministry leaders. We bring practical teaching on leadership development, pastoral health, and Kingdom-minded living to your church or event.

How Enhance Started

In the late months of 2020, the Holy Spirit ignited a profound realization in Matt's heart—an understanding that the first two decades of his ministry were a divine preparation for the next 20 years and beyond. Fueled by this revelation, Matt, a seasoned coach, team-builder, and speaker, took a bold step of faith and launched Enhance Ministries as a 501c3 non-profit. Since that pivotal moment, God has been at work using Matt's gifts, to advance the sustainability of pastors.

Matt is a highly gifted coach, leader and speaker. With over 20 years of experience, he coached dozens of leaders towards greater effectiveness, deeper clarity, and positive reliance on Jesus. As a passionate believer in changing the world through Kingdom-minded living, Matt brings a unique perspective and practical insights that encourage and challenge leaders to excel for the 'long-haul' of ministry calling, operating fully within their gifting and passions.
