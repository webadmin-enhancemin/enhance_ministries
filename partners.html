Kingdom Partnerships

Ministry Partners

Enhance Ministries endorses these ministries, recognizing the Kingdom and Body of Christ require diverse support structures for different seasons.

The Center for Biblical Living

The Center for Biblical Living (CBL) is dedicated to providing biblical counseling for ministry leaders. They serve pastors, missionaries, church staff, seminary students and professors, and Christian organization leaders in the U.S. and around the world. CBL desires to strengthen the impact of the church by providing biblical care and counseling for ministers so that they will be effective and fruitful in leading their ministries and living the gospel before their families, churches, and communities.

GodTown

We're honored to partner with GodTown, a ministry committed to equipping believers to transform communities through intentional discipleship and hands-on outreach. Based in St. Paul, GodTown empowers individuals and teams to grow in their faith, engage in neighborhood service projects, and build lasting relationships through prayer, worship, community gardens, food distribution, children's ministry, and more. Whether hosting local mission teams or providing leadership training that activates believers in their calling, GodTown exemplifies Kingdom-centered impact in both urban spaces and global contexts.

Visit Website

Good in the Hood

We're proud to partner with Good in the Hood, a community-focused nonprofit dedicated to changing lives together with simple acts of kindness. Rooted in intentional service and neighborhood engagement, Good in the Hood fosters connection, dignity, and hope by providing essential resources like food, shoes, wellness support, and holiday assistance, while creating volunteer opportunities that bring people together in meaningful ways. Through food programs, outreach programs, and kindness-based initiatives, they work to uplift individuals, strengthen families, and build healthier, more compassionate communities.

Visit Website

Source MN

We're honored to partner with Source MN, a long-standing Christian ministry in Minneapolis that empowers the at-risk, unreached, and vulnerable through holistic urban outreach. For over three decades, Source MN has met both physical and spiritual needs through programs like food distribution, community meals, English classes, supportive housing, anti-trafficking outreach, women's drop-in support, and relational care, all grounded in sharing the hope of Christ with dignity and compassion. Their work reflects a bold commitment to breaking cycles of poverty and hopelessness while building foundations for a stronger future in the heart of the city.

Visit Website

Hearts & Hammers

We're grateful to partner with Hearts & Hammers, a hands-on ministry transforming lives through compassionate home repair and service. By providing critical repairs, accessibility modifications, and physical care for those in need especially seniors, veterans, and families facing hardship, Hearts & Hammers demonstrates the love of Christ in practical, life-changing ways. Their work builds safer, more stable homes while fostering dignity, community connection, and hope. Together with Hearts & Hammers, we're helping neighbors experience restoration from the inside out.

Visit Website

The Chap Store

We're proud to partner with The Chap Store, a ministry devoted to equipping and encouraging pastors, ministry leaders, and their families with resources that inspire spiritual growth and personal well-being. Through curated books, devotionals, apparel, and gifts crafted specifically for those in ministry, The Chap Store brings encouragement straight to the heart of the church. Their passion for supporting ministry leaders aligns with our commitment at Enhance to uplift, strengthen, and walk alongside pastors in every season of leadership.
