Popular Topics

Speaking Themes

Matt offers a variety of engaging topics that can be customized to fit your event's needs and audience.

Overcoming Lazy River Faith

Challenge passive Christianity and discover what it means to actively pursue God's calling with intentionality and passion.

Missional Living

Learn how to live on mission every day, right where you are, turning ordinary moments into Kingdom opportunities.

Love Recklessly

Explore what it means to love others the way Jesus loves us—without limits, without conditions, and without fear.

Lead Courageously

Develop the courage to lead with conviction, navigate uncertainty, and make bold decisions that honor God.

Resting in the Lord

Discover the spiritual discipline of rest and learn to find peace and renewal in God's presence.

Faith When It Doesn't Make Sense

Navigate seasons of doubt, grief, and uncertainty while holding onto hope and trusting God's faithfulness.

Pillar vs. Monument

Understand the difference between building something that supports others versus something that glorifies self.
